# **FEATURES**

## **A. CUSTOMER FEATURES**

* **A1. Search & Discovery:** Comprehensive vehicle search with filters, sorting, comparison, and detailed listing pages.
* **A2. Booking & Reservation:** A multi-step booking process for selecting dates, vehicles, add-ons, and completing secure payment.
* **A3. User Account & Profile:** A personal dashboard for managing bookings, favorites, profile settings, and viewing history.
* **A4. Payment & Billing:** Secure, multi-method payment processing with automated invoicing and refund management.
* **A5. Reviews & Ratings:** A verified review system for customers to rate agencies and vehicles post-rental.
* **A6. Customer Support:** A centralized help center with FAQs, contact forms, and dedicated booking assistance.

## **B. AGENCY FEATURES**

* **B1. Agency Registration & Verification:** A streamlined onboarding process with document upload and admin verification.
* **B2. Vehicle/Inventory Management:** A complete system for adding, editing, and managing vehicle listings, pricing, and availability.
* **B3. Booking Management:** A dashboard for agencies to view, confirm, and manage all customer bookings and communication.
* **B4. Financial Management:** Tools for tracking earnings, commissions, payouts, and generating financial reports.
* **B5. Agency Profile & Settings:** Management of public profile, business settings, and team member access.
* **B6. Analytics & Insights:** Performance dashboards with key metrics on bookings, revenue, and customer insights.

## **C. ADMINISTRATOR FEATURES**

* **C1. Platform Management:** An admin dashboard for overseeing all platform users, agencies, and global operations.
* **C2. Agency Verification:** An interface for reviewing, approving, or rejecting new agency registrations and documents.
* **C3. Content Moderation:** Tools for moderating user-generated content like reviews and listings, and managing complaints.
* **C4. Platform Configuration:** Centralized control over system settings, commission rates, email templates, and policies.
* **C5. Analytics & Reporting:** Comprehensive platform-wide analytics for traffic, financials, and business performance.
* **C6. Marketing & Growth Tools:** Features to run promotional campaigns, manage featured listings, and execute email marketing.

## **D. TECHNICAL FEATURES**

* **D1. User Experience:** A responsive, multi-language, accessible, and high-performance web application.
* **D2. Security Features:** Comprehensive data protection, secure authentication, privacy compliance, and fraud prevention.
* **D3. Search & Discovery Technology:** Smart search algorithms, recommendation engines, and integrated location services.
* **D4. Notification System:** A multi-channel system for automated email, SMS, and in-app notifications.
* **D5. Data Management:** Robust systems for data backup, export, and database optimization.
